About Recipe Creator


Our group has developed an application that suggests recipes for the user to make. The idea is that the user will input ingredients and food items that they have available to them at the time. While the user inputs their ingredients, the autocomplete function will shows potential suggestions for what the user may be inputting. In return, the app will search through a database of recipes, find recipes that use most (if not all) of the ingredients the user input, and return those recipes. If the recipe requires more ingredients, the user will be notified. The user will have the option of creating an account to store the history of their queries, favorite recipes for future reference, and potentially make posts to suggest recipes to other users. For the public data store, any user will be allowed to query for recipes and submit other recipes for the database. For the private data store, users will have the benefit of making posts reviewing recipes that they have tried as well as interacting with other posts made. For the user data store, users will be able to adjust their login information, view their favorites and posts, and set their recipes preferences.
